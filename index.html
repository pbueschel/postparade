The problem

Placing a horse takes a whole afternoon.

Reading condition books. Cross-checking eligibility. Comparing purse value. Checking jockey availability. Coordinating shipping. It's all manual — and easy to miss the right spot.

  • Condition language is dense and inconsistent across tracks
  • One missed preference tier and your entry doesn't draw
  • Jockey conflicts only show up after you've committed
  • The right spot at another track gets missed entirely
How it works

Three steps. Then ranked spots, every morning.

1

Connect your stable

Import horses from Equibase, or add them manually. We sync past performances, workouts, and earnings automatically.

2

We read every condition book

Every track on your circuit, every night. Conditions get parsed into structured eligibility rules — preferences, weight allowances, all of it.

3

You get ranked spots

For each horse, your top 5 races — with eligibility, jockey availability, projected field, and shipping logistics all checked.

Features

Built around the work you already do.

Eligibility

Every condition, checked.

Age, sex, prior wins, claim history, state restrictions, preference tiers — all validated against each horse's record automatically. If they qualify, you know. If they don't, you know why.

  • Catches preference-tier qualifiers other tools miss
  • Flags weight allowances you'd otherwise calculate by hand
  • Shows the rule next to the pass/fail — auditable, not magic

Parser

Condition books, parsed.

Modern LLMs read condition language better than any rules engine ever could. We extract ~45 structured fields per race, validate against jockey-club rules, and flag anything ambiguous for human review.

  • Every track, every night, end-to-end
  • 98% confidence on standard conditions
  • Human-in-the-loop on the edge cases
