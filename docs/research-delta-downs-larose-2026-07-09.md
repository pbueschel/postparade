## Kinnon LaRose — who he is

Kinnon LaRose, 28, grew up in Ogdensburg, NY, making regular family trips to
Saratoga. He played Division I basketball at Siena College then Sacred Heart
University, then joined Tom Amoss's stable as a hot walker in October 2020 after
emailing Amoss directly. He worked up through groom, foreman, and six years as
Amoss's head assistant before taking over as head trainer of the former Amoss
operation on April 1, 2026, when Amoss moved to an advisory/broadcasting role
(the app displays this as just "Kinnon LaRose" — the earlier "Kinnon LaRose
Racing Stables" label in this doc and in the app was never an independently
verified business name, corrected 2026-07-09). As of the meet notes below,
the stable runs
~60 horses: 28 at Churchill Downs, 8 at Churchill Downs' Trackside Training
Center, and 32 stalls at Keeneland.

## Delta Downs — why the *current* meet is Quarter Horse, not Thoroughbred

Delta Downs (Vinton, LA) runs a fall/winter Thoroughbred meet (~Oct/Nov–Feb)
and a spring/summer Quarter Horse meet (~Apr–Jul). As of today (July 9, 2026),
the live meet is **Quarter Horse racing**, April 24 – July 18, 2026 (46 days).
The prior Thoroughbred meet (Oct 10, 2025 – Feb 21, 2026, 76 days) already
concluded — Allen Landry won leading trainer (his 2nd straight title, 40
wins/194 starts); that's a *different*, Thoroughbred-only trainer population
and wasn't used in the build, since it's not what's live now. Kinnon LaRose
has no real Quarter Horse connection — his one 2026 Delta Downs runner
(Authentic Gallop, Feb 20 win) was during that concluded Thoroughbred meet.

Track: 6-furlong dirt (sand/clay) oval, 550-yard QH chute, 1200-horse
stabling.

### Quarter Horse class vocabulary (for the demo's QH classLadder)

Maiden (MDN) · Claiming (Clm) · Allowance (Alw) · Stakes, graded G1/G2/G3 ·
**Restricted Graded RG1/RG2/RG3** (state-bred-restricted — the QH-distinctive
category, e.g. "Louisiana-bred") · **Futurity** (2yo, breeder/owner-funded) ·
**Derby** (3yo equivalent) · **Trial** — qualifying heats that precede a
futurity/derby final, since fields of 60–100+ can't run in one heat; fastest
times advance a fixed number (often 10) to the final. Distances are always
stated in **yards**, never furlongs (this repo's existing `furlongs()` display
helper doesn't apply to Delta Downs races).

Source: [AQHA — What Are the Types of Quarter Horse Races?](https://www.aqha.com/-/what-are-the-types-of-quarter-horse-races-)

## Architecture implications for the build

1. **tour.html back-compat (CLAUDE.md hard rule 2) is non-negotiable.**
   tour.html dynamically loads the live `app/data.js`/`app/engine.js` at
   runtime (it is not a fully frozen data snapshot) and its own frozen markup
   hardcodes references to `PPData.meet`/`PPData.race` and specific Churchill
   Downs entities. **The `cd-2026-summer` meet, its anchor race, and every
   horse currently seeded stay in `app/data.js` untouched** — nothing is
   deleted or renamed. "Replacing Churchill Downs as the demo's meet" is
   implemented by changing what's *featured* (the Trainer workspace's demo
   persona, the Track workspace's default landing content) — not by deleting
   the underlying CD data tour.html depends on.
2. **The demo trainer persona swap** is a one-line change: flip which stable
   has `isDemoUser: true` from `snellgrove` to a new `larose` stable. The old
   Snellgrove stable and horses stay in the seed (satisfies tour.html + keeps
   Churchill Downs' race fill-counts intact) but are no longer the featured
   persona anywhere in the live app.
3. **Quarter Horse support is new engine/display surface, not just data.**
   Distance must display in yards for Delta Downs races (the existing
   `furlongs()` helper doesn't apply), and the classLadder vocabulary needs a
   QH-specific set (MDN/Clm/Alw/Stk-G1-3/RG1-3/Futurity/Derby/Trial) alongside
   the existing Thoroughbred one — used only for Delta Downs races, leaving
   every existing Thoroughbred screen/table unchanged.
